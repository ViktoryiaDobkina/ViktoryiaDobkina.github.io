Things I am Passionate about

My interests in life were always focused on three things: traveling, photography, and theater.

Travelling

My travelling experiences began at a really young age and until now have never stopped. Despite the fact that, in recent years, COVID hit hard, I still find ways to travel, and I am already exploring new places to visit.

Photography

Speaking of photography, I both like to take a photo and be in the photo itself. Most of all, I enjoy the creative part of the process and can spend hours implementing the idea.

Theatre

Finally, the part of my life I couldn't imagine myself without is theatre. I attended drama club for six years and played diverse roles, such as Orchied, the king, teacher, and etc.


The theatre as a place significantly influenced me as the person I am today and formed a strong feeling of how community should be felt. Therefore, I was looking for the same atmosphere as I perused in this chapter of my life, and I found it in the university I am at right now.
Here you can learn about the experience I am going through.
