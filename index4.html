My University Life

I study at LCC International University, which is located in Lithuania, Klaipeda. My major is contemporary communications, and right now is my sophomore year (Fall, 2022). As a freshman, I was excited to experience such a new, diverse culture as we have at LCC. Therefore, I got acquainted and became friends with people of different nationalities and backgrounds. I found a second family at university, which became my second home. To be clear, we did create a so-called "family" on the floor I was living on the previous year. Though we are all pretty much in different countries right now, this is something that is always going to stay in my heart.

Speaking of my university life this year, I am taking a leadership position(Fyrst Year Seminar Leader) and helping freshmen in their transition process to university.
